# Expense Management System - Contextual Information

## Company Expense Policy

### Expense Categories

1. **Travel**
   - Train, bus, taxi, and flight expenses
   - Mileage claims (company car or personal vehicle)
   - Parking fees
   - Maximum taxi fare: £100 per journey (requires manager approval above this)

2. **Meals**
   - Business meals with clients or colleagues
   - Lunch allowance: £15 per day
   - Dinner allowance: £25 per day
   - Alcohol: Maximum 2 drinks, must be with client

3. **Supplies**
   - Office stationery and supplies
   - Computer accessories
   - Books and training materials
   - Maximum: £200 per month without pre-approval

4. **Accommodation**
   - Hotel stays for business travel
   - Maximum rate: £150 per night in London, £100 elsewhere
   - 4-star or below only
   - Breakfast included where possible

5. **Other**
   - Conference fees
   - Professional memberships
   - Any other business-related expenses
   - Requires manager approval

### Approval Process

1. **Draft**: Employee creates expense claim
2. **Submitted**: Employee submits for manager review
3. **Approved**: Manager approves the expense
4. **Rejected**: Manager rejects with reason

### Submission Requirements

- All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date
- Original receipts required for amounts over £25
- Digital receipts acceptable for online purchases
- Description must clearly state business purpose
- VAT receipts required for amounts over £100

### Reimbursement Timeline

- Approved expenses are paid within 5 working days
- Payment made via bank transfer to registered account
- Check payslip for expense reimbursement line item

## Common Questions

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Managers typically review and approve expenses within 2-3 business days.

**Q: Can I claim for coffee/snacks during working hours?**
A: Only if attending a business meeting or working late (after 7pm).

**Q: What if I lose a receipt?**
A: For amounts under £25, a written explanation may be accepted. Over £25 requires receipt.

**Q: Can I submit expenses from last year?**
A: No, expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date.

**Q: What currencies are accepted?**
A: All expenses must be in GBP. Foreign expenses should be converted at the time of purchase.
